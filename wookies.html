About Wookies

Wookies are a species of tall, hairy humanoids from the planet Kashyyyk. They are known for their strength, loyalty, and distinctive vocalizations.

Famous Wookies

  • Chewbacca - The most famous Wookie, co-pilot of the Millennium Falcon.
  • Lumpawaroo - A Wookie who fought in the Clone Wars.
  • Tarfful - A Wookie chieftain who aided the Rebel Alliance.
